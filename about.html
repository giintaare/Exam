Iri

Irizan Imamova is a hand-made jewellery artist. Since 2011 she is creating her pieces of art under her own brand "Iri Jewellery". The idea of creating jewellery came spontaniosly: wathing a TV show. Since then she is creating jewels almost everyday. Recent years most all of her works are made with Swarovski.
